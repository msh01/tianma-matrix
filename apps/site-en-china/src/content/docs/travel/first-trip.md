title: "First Trip to China: What to Prepare"
description: "A concise guide to payments, transportation, connectivity, etiquette, and daily practical details for first-time visitors."
---

A first trip to China is easier when a few practical details are handled before arrival: mobile payment, maps, translation, local transportation, and basic etiquette.

Visitors should check whether their payment method works, install useful apps, prepare offline information for addresses, and learn a few common phrases. Most friction comes from daily logistics, not from sightseeing itself.

This section will grow into a practical travel knowledge base rather than a travel diary.
